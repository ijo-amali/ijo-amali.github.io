> go north_

That is the name under which I release music. It's probably the project I put the most amount of regular work into. Music is a great way to abstractly represent perception and feeling, and so I usually don't accompany my compositions with lyrics.

Genre: semi-experiental electronica for careful listening, with a focus on textures and instrumentation

So far, I've released three albums and two singles. The most recent is called Cheers, Big Ears! and I'm quite proud of it. You can find my music on lots of different music platforms (the different links can be found by typing "go north" into the > go north_ website linked here!). Bandcamp is preferred, and can be found here.
